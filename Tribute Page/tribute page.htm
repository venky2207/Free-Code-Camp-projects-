Nelson Mandela

Former President of South Africa

Achievements of Nelson Mandela

  • 1950 - HE BECAME THE NATIONAL PRESIDENT OF THE ANC YOUTH LEAGUE
  • 1952 - MANDELA WAS A LEADING OPPONENT OF THE 1948 APARTHEID LEGISLATION
  • 1953 - HE CO-FOUNDED THE FIRST BLACK LEGAL PARTNERSHIP IN SOUTH AFRICA
  • 1961 - HIS SPEECH DURING RIVONIA TRIAL IS CONSIDERED ONE OF THE GREAT SPEECHES OF THE 20TH CENTURY
  • 1990 - NELSON MANDELA PLAYED THE LEADING ROLE IN ENDING APARTHEID
  • 1994 - NELSON MANDELA WAS THE FIRST BLACK PRESIDENT OF SOUTH AFRICA
  • 1995 - HE FOCUSED ON NATIONAL RECONCILIATION DURING HIS PRESIDENCY
  • 1998 -HE SERVED AS THE SECRETARY-GENERAL OF THE NON-ALIGNED MOVEMENT
  • 1999 - HE FOUNDED A NUMBER OF ORGANIZATIONS TO PROMOTE EQUALITY AND FIGHT PROBLEMS LIKE AIDS
  • 1993 - NELSON MANDELA WAS AWARDED THE 1993 NOBEL PEACE PRIZE
